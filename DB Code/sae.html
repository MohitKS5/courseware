coupling

rigid coupling

for alighned shafts both laterally and angular

types

sleeve

  • as the name suggests a sleeve passes over both shafts and are angularly fixed by keys
  • PRINCIPLE:groove locking

clamp<

  • 2 half cylindrical clamps clamped by through bolts like semicircle on center of a line
  • PRINCIPLE:based on tight fit principal clearly

flange

  • two disks with shaft passing from each(1 in each) and keyed. Now the two disks clamped by through bolts

flexible coupling

for unalighned shafts

types

bush pin

  • bolts are fitted with flexible bushes
  • for slight lateral misalignment
  • range is less

Universal coupling

  • for large angular misalignment basically ,may handle slight lateral also
  • 2 parts:cylinders fitted with tabs on diametrically opposite directions
  • 2 parts are oriented in such a way the axis of tabs are perpendicular ,then a plus shaped assembly is inserted between fixed by collar pin fasteners .The shafts are fixed by keys .
  • it allows a misalignment of about 25 degrees
  • used in propeller shaft of automobiles as a CV joint

oldham coupling

  • for lateral misalignments __
  • 3 part system - 2 metal and a plastic,2 disk shaped metal have a diametric groove. and a disk shaped plastic with external/extruded bits to fit into the grooves.
  • the metals are misaligned and plastic is pressed in between , so it cant handle axial outward forces as the plastic will slip off .
  • well its made of plastic because it wears of due to shear and plastic can be replaced easily
